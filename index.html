Our Services

Specialized Departments

We offer dedicated departments for Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics, Dermatology, ENT, and more—each staffed by experienced specialists.

Diagnostics & Imaging

Our facility includes advanced diagnostics such as Digital X-ray, 3D Ultrasound, MRI, CT Scan, and full pathology labs for accurate test results.

Inpatient & Surgery Care

Fully equipped operation theatres, ICU, and recovery rooms. We handle both major surgeries and minor procedures with round-the-clock support.

Health Camps & Outreach

We organize regular free health camps, vaccination drives, mobile clinics, and awareness programs for rural and urban communities alike.

Telemedicine & Online Care

Consult doctors online, book appointments, access e-prescriptions, and download lab reports—all from the comfort of your home.

Patient Amenities

On-campus pharmacy, cafeteria, visitor lounges, ambulance services, and insurance support ensure a comfortable experience for patients and families.
