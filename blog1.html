5 Tips for a Healthy Heart

By Dr. A. Kumar | May 10, 2025

Your heart deserves the best care. Follow these tips to keep it strong:

  1. Stay Active: Engage in 30 mins of brisk walking or exercise daily.
  2. Eat Smart: Include omega-3 rich foods and avoid trans fats.
  3. Control Stress: Practice mindfulness and breathing exercises.
  4. Limit Alcohol: Reduce intake and avoid binge drinking.
  5. Regular Checkups: Monitor cholesterol and blood pressure.

Make small changes today to live a long, heart-healthy life!
