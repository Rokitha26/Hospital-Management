Nutrition Tips from Doctors

By Dr. Riya Sharma | May 10, 2025

What do top doctors recommend for staying fit and energized?

  • Stay Hydrated: Drink at least 8 glasses of water a day.
  • Eat Whole Foods: Choose natural, unprocessed items.
  • Limit Sugar: Cut down on soft drinks and sweets.
  • Smart Snacking: Replace chips with fruits or nuts.
  • Balanced Meals: Each plate should have carbs, protein & veggies.
